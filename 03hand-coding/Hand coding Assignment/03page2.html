Oven-Bake Clay Guide

How to Use Oven-Bake Clay

  • Step 1: Sculpt Your Creation
  • Step 2: Bake the Clay

Step by step guide

Step 1

  1. Conditioning the Clay: Take your block of clay and knead it between your hands to soften it. If the clay is too hard, you can warm it slightly by rolling it or use a clay softener.
  2. Shaping and Tools: shape the clay into your desired form using your hands or tools.
  3. Smoothing the Surface: To remove fingerprints or imperfections, use a small amount of rubbing alcohol on a soft brush or a smoothing tool to gently polish the surface. This ensures a professional finish before baking.

Step 2:

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to the recommended temperature on your clay packaging (usually between 265°F and 275°F).
  2. Prepare the Baking Surface: Place your clay piece on a ceramic tile, parchment paper, or a dedicated baking tray to prevent the clay from sticking or burning.
  3. Bake Time: The baking time depends on the thickness of your project. A general rule is to bake for 15-30 minutes per 1/4 inch (6mm) of thickness. More baking information.

Recommended supplies

Oven bake clay
Basic Sculpting Tools
Glaze or Sealer
Paint for small deatails after you bake (optional)
