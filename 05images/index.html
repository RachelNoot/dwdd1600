Introduction to Oven-Bake Clay

Oven-bake clay, also known as polymer clay, is a versatile and accessible medium, perfect for crafters of all levels. It’s used to create everything from simple beads to intricate sculptures. This guide will take you through everything you need to know, from basics to expert tips.

Popular Projects with Oven-Bake Clay

  • Jewelry: Earrings, necklaces, bracelets.
  • Home Décor: Custom coasters, picture frames, ornaments.
  • Miniatures: Figurines, tiny food replicas.
  • Gifts: Personalized keychains, magnets, and more.

The History of Oven-Bake Clay

Polymer clay was originally developed in the early 20th century as a material for insulating wires, but its versatility as a crafting material was soon discovered by artists. Over time, it has evolved into a key medium for artists worldwide.
